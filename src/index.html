QRTY

QR Code Decoder. Capture a section of your screen to scan QR codes, or upload an image.

Capture a section of your screen to scan QR codes, or upload an image file containing a QR code. The interface stays minimal so you can easily scan QR codes from your desktop.

Screen capture: Clicking "Capture Screen" will request permission to capture your screen or a window. QR codes will be automatically detected from the captured screen.

Privacy: All QR code processing happens entirely in your browser. No images, videos, or data are sent to any server or stored anywhere. Your privacy is protected.

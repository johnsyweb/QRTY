Why QRTY?

Ever received a QR code in an email but couldn't use it because it's just an image, not a clickable link? QRTY solves this by converting QR codes from images into usable URLs that you can click, copy, or share.

Whether you receive QR codes via email, screenshots, or any other image format, QRTY processes them entirely in your browser (no data leaves your device) and gives you immediate access to the URLs or data they contain.

Capture a section of your screen or upload an image file containing a single QR or barcode code to decode. For best results, ensure only one code is visible in your capture. The interface stays minimal so you can easily copy the results.

Privacy: All code processing happens entirely in your browser. No images, videos, or data are sent to any server or stored anywhere. Your privacy is protected.
